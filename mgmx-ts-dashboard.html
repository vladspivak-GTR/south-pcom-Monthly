✓
GSM is untouchable at $72.1K — flat profit on 72% more spend. ROI compressed from 12,952% to 7,496% but still absurdly high. GSM is the most capital-efficient source. Top geos: PL ($12.2K), NL ($11.5K), DE ($11.0K), UK ($7.5K).
✓
11 new WL/BL sources launched and generated $108K+ combined profit — more than half of total. PRX-WL ($23.3K), PA-IOS-3G-WL ($20.7K), PA-IOS-3G-BL ($18.2K), PRX-BL ($15.9K) are the top four. This diversification is the biggest March story.
⚠
Legacy PRX collapsed -79% ($85.5K→$17.8K). This was Feb's #1 source. Spend dropped from $7.2K to $1.6K. The volume clearly migrated to PRX-WL and PRX-BL variants. Combined PRX family (PRX+WL+BL) = $56.9K — still down from $85.5K.
⚠
PA-IOS-3G fell -61% ($36.4K→$14.1K) but ROI actually improved (934%→1,392%). The WL/BL splits absorbed volume: PA-IOS-3G family total = $53.0K vs Feb's $36.4K — actually net +$16.6K across the family.
⚡
Action: Scale GSM from $962 to $2K+ — even at 7,496% ROI, there's massive headroom. Optimize PRX-WL geo mix (US $10.8K at 4,345% ROI is the standout). Test PA-IOS-3G-WL in more geos — it's currently concentrated in PL ($13.4K).
⚡
Action: Monitor iCloud-BL efficiency — $2.4K profit but only 248% ROI on $969 spend. Compare with iCloud-WL (328% ROI) and original iCloud (1,563% ROI). The BL variant may need optimization before further scaling.
