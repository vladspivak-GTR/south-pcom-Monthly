📖 The 2026 Story · Jan → May

May held strong at $386.4K profit — essentially flat vs April's record $392.7K (-2%), the second-best month of the year. Profit has stayed above $386K for two straight months after the February dip ($272.8K).
The MGMX → Yenoti migration completed in May. Yenoti now carries ~88% of profit ($338.9K, +64% MoM) while MGMX deliberately wound down to $47.6K (-74%). The portfolio flipped from roughly 50/50 to Yenoti-led in a single month.
CR doubled to 0.10% (from 0.05%). With half the visit volume (106M vs 228M) but the same ~109K conversions, traffic quality jumped sharply — exactly the point of consolidating everything onto Yenoti.
ROI eased to 794% — cost rose +25% to $48.6K as Yenoti scaled while profit held. That's the trade-off of moving onto the higher-cost, higher-quality platform; absolute profit, which matters most, stayed near the YTD high.
YTD profit: $1.77M across five months (avg $353K/mo). On pace for a $4.2M year.
